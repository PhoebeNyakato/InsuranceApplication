Problem Statement and how to solve.: 

Insurance policy administration involves managing the legal contract (life insurance policy) between the insurer and the policyholder throughout the policy lifecycleâ€”from policy issuance, premium collection, endorsements, renewals, to claims and termination.

In many insurance companies, particularly in developing markets, policy administration is characterized by manual processes, fragmented data storage, and heavy paperwork.

To address these challenges, the implementation of an Insurance Policy Administration System (IPAS) is proposed. The system will automate and integrate all policy-related processes into a single digital platform.


The system will entail:

1. Centralized Data Management

Store all policyholder, policy, premium, and beneficiary data in a single secure database

Ensure data consistency and eliminate duplication

Enable easy retrieval and updating of policy records



2. Automation of Policy Lifecycle Processes

Automated policy creation and issuance

Automatic premium billing and reminders.

Automated policy renewals and cancellations.



3. Digital Documentation and Workflow

Replace physical paperwork with electronic policy documents

Enable electronic signatures for policy agreements
